Internet by optics

Our company offers high-speed Internet connection using Fiber-To-The-Building technology, which means using fiber-optic cable instead of copper. Connection via an optical communication channel is distinguished by such criteria as increased reliability and increased channel bandwidth, which plays a key role in choosing an Internet provider.
